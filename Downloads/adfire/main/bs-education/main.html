EASY TO OPERATE

Enter your classroom, call out the student's names, and at the click of the button, mark their attendance! This not only saves paper wastage, but also crucial errors which occur while marking attendance.

ASK THE EXPERT

CLASS-WISE,TEACHER-WISE AND SUBJECT-WISE ATTENDANCE

Each teacher marks the attendance for the class and for the subject which they are teaching. This means that you can search the attendance for a particular subject as well as a particular teacher.

ASK THE EXPERT

VERIFICATION BY STUDENTS

At the end of each academic month(preferrably) or at any time you desire, you may get the attendance data cross-checked by the students. This will make the system fair and just.
